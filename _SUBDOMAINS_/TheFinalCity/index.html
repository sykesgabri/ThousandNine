TheFinalCity

A Glasgow Band



TheFinalCity is a band from Glasgow, Scotland.
Their music is mostly rock and alt-rock stuff but they mess around with all sorts of genres.
The (Supposed To Be) 6-Person band consists of Jay Dignall, J.C. Anderson, Charlie McIntyre, Gabriel Sykes and Mark Watson.
